Overview: Rose in the June 2026 meta

Rose is the leader of the Smarty and Solar classes, and one of the game's premier control heroes. Her signature superpower, Goatify, turns the zombie with the highest strength into a harmless 1/1 Goat, and between healing, freezing, and stat-debuffs she's built to grind opponents into the ground rather than race them. Her Solar half also gives her some of the best flower synergy of any plant hero, which opens up ramp-style builds alongside the control lists.

That control identity dominates the top of this list: the best-scoring Rose decks are all variations of Midrose, the healing-midrange shell anchored by Aloesaurus, Brainana, Pepper M.D., and Cob Cannon. Heal Midrose leads at 91.5, with several near-identical variants right behind it. Lower down you'll find the bean-tribal Musical Fruit and the flower-ramp Shoot for the Star (a perfect 100 consistency) and Birb Ramp as off-meta alternatives. Rose doesn't have an S-tier deck this month — her best builds top out at A — but she remains a rock-solid ladder pick.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best Rose deck right now?

As of June 2026, Heal Midrose is the highest-rated Rose deck in our database with a 91.5 composite score, an A grade, and a 99 synergy rating. It's a healing midrange deck built around Aloesaurus, Brainana, and Cob Cannon.

What classes and playstyle does Rose have?

Rose leads the Smarty and Solar classes. Her signature superpower, Goatify, transforms the zombie with the highest strength into a 1/1 Goat. She's a control hero that wins through healing, freezing, debuffing zombie stats, and strong flower synergy.

Why are so many top Rose decks called "Midrose"?

Rose's strongest shell is healing midrange, nicknamed Midrose, so most of her best-scoring builds are variations on it. The list also includes a bean-tribal deck (Musical Fruit) and flower-ramp builds (Shoot for the Star and Birb Ramp) as alternatives.

Does Rose have an S-tier deck?

Not this month. Rose's best lists top out at an A grade, with Heal Midrose leading at 91.5. She's a strong control hero, but her current decks sit just below the S-tier cutoff.

How are these Rose decks ranked?

Each deck is scored across four pillars — performance, consistency, synergy, and curve health — then graded and ordered by composite value from our live user database.
