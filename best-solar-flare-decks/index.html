Overview: Solar Flare in the June 2026 meta

Solar Flare is the hero version of Sunflower and the leader of the Kabloom and Solar classes. Her signature superpower, Sunburn, does 2 damage and grants +1 sun for the rest of the game — a fitting emblem for a hero defined by ramp. By generating sun faster than anyone else, Solar Flare plays expensive payoffs like Cob Cannon and Dandy Lion King well ahead of schedule, then backs them with Kabloom burn (Berry Blast, Sizzle, Fireweed) and Solar healing (Aloesaurus, Astro Vera, Power Flower).

She is the strongest hero in the game right now, and it shows: four of her decks are S-tier, led by Fig Lottery — a 99.2-rated combo deck with perfect 100 synergy and power that tops every hero. Behind it sit a mushroom-and-Molekale value engine (suvk oengjt hub), the all-four-of Samen Burn, and the heal-leaning Happy LeapDay. The A-tier builds branch into heal control, strikethrough aggro, and mushroom combo, so there's a Solar Flare deck here for every playstyle.
