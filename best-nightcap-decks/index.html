Overview: Nightcap in the June 2026 meta

Nightcap is the leader of the Kabloom and Smarty classes, and one of the game's premier aggressive heroes. The signature superpower, Mush-Boom, drops a 1/1 Poison Mushroom and pings a zombie for 2, setting the tone for a hero built to close games quickly with cheap mushrooms, beans, and Kabloom burn like Berry Blast, Sizzle, and Fig. The Smarty half adds tempo and reach — Brainana, Winter Melon, and Shrinking Violet — to paper over Nightcap's traditional weakness in the late game.

The list splits into three families. At the top, Toyota Controlla is a tempo-control build with a perfect 100 synergy that leads the pack at 93.8. Below it sit the cycle decks — mushroom engines like Cycular Reasoning, cycle, and BLOOM Shakalaka that chain Astro Shroom, Admiral Navy Bean, and Planet of the Grapes for draw — and the Paradise aggro builds wrapped around Bird of Paradise and Pear's Paradise. Nightcap doesn't have an S-tier deck this month; the field tops out at A, with only Blowster's Paradise dropping to B.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best Nightcap deck right now?

As of June 2026, Toyota Controlla is the highest-rated Nightcap deck in our database with a 93.8 composite score, an A grade, and a perfect 100 synergy rating. It's a tempo-control build using Winter Melon, Sizzle, and Shrinking Violet alongside Fig and Brainana value.

What classes and playstyle does Nightcap have?

Nightcap leads the Kabloom and Smarty classes. The signature superpower, Mush-Boom, makes a 1/1 Poison Mushroom and deals 2 damage to a zombie in that lane. Nightcap is a fast, hyper-aggressive tempo hero built on cheap mushrooms, beans, and Kabloom burn, and tends to struggle in the late game.

What are the "cycle" and "Paradise" decks?

The cycle decks are mushroom engines that chain cheap cards with Astro Shroom, Admiral Navy Bean, and Planet of the Grapes for draw. The Paradise decks are aggressive Kabloom builds wrapped around Bird of Paradise and Pear's Paradise — several are named as riffs on the same theme.

Does Nightcap have an S-tier deck?

Not this month. Nightcap's best lists top out at an A grade, with Toyota Controlla leading at 93.8. Nightcap is a strong aggressive hero, but the current builds sit just below the S-tier cutoff.
