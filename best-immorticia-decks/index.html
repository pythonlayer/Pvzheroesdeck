Overview: Immorticia in the June 2026 meta

Immorticia pairs the Brainy and Beastly zombie classes, which makes her the undisputed queen of teleport tricks in Plants vs. Zombies Heroes. Every deck in this top 10 is built around that identity in a different way: cheating big bodies into play on the tricks phase, growing snowballing threats like Cat Lady and Zom-Blob, or assembling one-turn-kill bursts the plant hero never gets to answer.

This month, the OTK shells lead the pack — OTK Cat Lady takes the crown at a 97.2 composite score, with the closely related BASTET just behind it. Slower, bruising teleport packages like GargTicia and Kaleidoscope round out the A-tier with raw power scores of 100.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best Immorticia deck right now?

As of June 2026, OTK Cat Lady is the highest-rated Immorticia deck in our database with a 97.2 composite score. It grows Cat Lady with repeated teleport tricks before converting her into a single lethal burst turn.

What classes does Immorticia use?

Immorticia combines the Brainy and Beastly zombie classes, giving her teleport tricks, science synergies, bounce effects, and beastly stat manipulation in one hero.

How are these Immorticia decks ranked?

Are these decks good for climbing the ranked ladder?
