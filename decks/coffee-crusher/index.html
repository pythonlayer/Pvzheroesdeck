Overview & Win Condition

Coffee Crusher utilizes Rustbolt's massive brain-generation engine to drop heavy, match-ending threats turns ahead of schedule. The core strategy revolves around establishing an Intergalactic Warlord to permanently buff your collection, then transitioning directly into an unstoppable trick-phase OTK push via Going Viral or positioning a massive Shieldcrusher Viking directly into open lanes.

Mulligan Strategy

Your opening phase choices dictate whether you run away with tempo or get buried early by aggressive curves.

🟢

Always Keep

Mustache Waxer and Teleportation Zombie. These establish immediate early presence and trigger baseline ramp mechanics.

⚠️

Vs. Aggro

Rolling Stone. Crucial for trading directly up into high-threat engine plays like Tricarrotops or early cycle elements.

Turn-By-Turn Sequence

Early Setup (Turns 1-3): Focus entirely on passive development and scaling resource structures. Drop Mustache Waxer to block out low-tier damage while generating excess banks. Rely heavily on text-removal tools like Rolling Stone to maintain parity.

Mid-Game Compression (Turns 4-5): Drop your Turquoise Skull Zombie onto optimal lanes to drain incoming solar equity directly from the Plant side. On Turn 5, commit entirely to your Intergalactic Warlord lines to anchor stat buffs across the remainder of your deck stack.

Lethal Execution (Turns 6+): Loop successive iterations of Going Viral across wide assemblies. Alternatively, leverage Trick-phase teleports to land an un-blockable Viking hit or seal standard damage paths with a comprehensive Coffee Zombie layout.
