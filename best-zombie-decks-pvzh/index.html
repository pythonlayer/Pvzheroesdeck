How the best Zombie decks are ranked

PvZH Vault evaluates each unique 2026 deck through the same scoring model, then orders the results by overall composite score.

⚔

Power

Estimates the strength and popularity of the cards used, with adjustments designed to separate proven staples from weaker inclusions.

♻

Consistency

Rewards focused 3x and 4x card packages and penalizes lists that depend too heavily on scattered single copies.

🔗

Synergy

Measures how frequently cards support one another across the wider deck database, including established engines and archetype cores.

📈

Curve health

Checks whether the deck can make productive plays throughout the game instead of clustering too heavily at awkward costs.

Scores are a deck-composition rating, not a published tournament win rate. Duplicate card-for-card lists are removed before ranking, and only decks with a 2026 upload date are eligible for this page.
