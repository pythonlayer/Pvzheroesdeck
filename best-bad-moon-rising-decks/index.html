Overview: Bad Moon Rising in the June 2026 meta

Bad Moon Rising is the Brainy trick that defines a whole family of zombie decks: a 7-brain event that transforms every zombie on the field into random zombies costing 5 or more. Flood the lawn with cheap, disposable bodies, drop the trick, and a row of throwaway one-drops leaps into a wall of giants in a single click. Every deck in this top 10 is a different machine for getting there — ramping the brains to cast it early, teleporting extra fodder onto the board, or stacking it on top of other transform effects so something is always upgrading.

This month the archetype is led by the A-tier, with no deck quite cracking into S. Ramp Moon Feasting on Professor Brainstorm takes the crown at a 90.5 composite score with a perfect 100 power rating, while Rustbolt's Gentleman's Leap and the Super Brainz / Huge-Gigantacus pair — Wormhole Snacekeeper and Dead Moon Rising — round out the top four. Because Super Brainz and Huge-Gigantacus lead the same Brainy and Sneaky classes, several lists here run on either hero.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best Bad Moon Rising deck right now?

As of June 2026, Ramp Moon Feasting on Professor Brainstorm is the highest-rated Bad Moon Rising deck in our database with a 90.5 composite score, an A grade, and a perfect 100 power rating. It ramps the brains, floods the board, transforms everything into giants, then closes with Gargantuars' Feast.

What does Bad Moon Rising do?

Bad Moon Rising is a Brainy class zombie trick. It costs 7 brains and transforms every zombie on the field into random zombies costing 5 or more, which is why it anchors so many flood-the-board-then-transform finisher decks.

Which heroes use Bad Moon Rising?

Bad Moon Rising is a Brainy card. The decks in this guide span four Brainy zombie heroes: Professor Brainstorm, Rustbolt, Super Brainz, and Huge-Gigantacus. Super Brainz and Huge-Gigantacus lead the same Brainy and Sneaky classes, so their lists are fully interchangeable.

How are these Bad Moon Rising decks ranked?

Each deck is scored across four pillars — performance, consistency, synergy, and curve health — then graded and ordered by composite value drawn from our live user database.

How many cards are in a PvZH deck?

A Plants vs. Zombies Heroes deck contains exactly 40 cards, with a maximum of 4 copies of any single card. Every Bad Moon Rising list in this guide follows that format.
